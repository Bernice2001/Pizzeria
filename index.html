ABOUT US

B PIZZERIA has been in business since 2000. Owner is a certified pizzaiolo of the Association of Africana Pizza-makers, and all of our pizzas are made according to Neapolitan tradition. We use only the highest-quality ingredients, including buffalo-milk mozzarella and tomatoes from our local shambas(garden)as well as our fresh, home-made mozzarella. All pizzas are drizzled with 100% Africana extra-virgin olive oil and then baked to perfection at 900 degrees in a Mario Acunto wood-fired brick oven. The finished product is a pizza whose taste will bring you back to Africa in KENYA!. Our distinctly Kenyan pizzas have been loved by the young, old and peckish for over 10 years.
